Nutrition

Change Your Mindset

  • Understand that life depends on a "healthy style" and not a "diet".
  • Making sports isn't enough to make the full change, train your brain(read more books for examples).


Adopt New Habits

Create a daily routine to stay active:
  • Manage your time and make a schedule for your tasks to be done.
  • Insert habits through your day t0 improve your health, like doing sports for 20 minutes every morning.


Eat Right

  • Increase the amount of proteins and fibers in your meal.
  • Avoid sugars and moderate carbohydrate intake.
  • Stay hydrated.
